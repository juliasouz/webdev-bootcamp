Julia's Chickpea Paste (Hummus) Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups of cooked and peeled chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon of tahini (sesame paste)
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons of minced garlic
  • 2 ladles of chickpea cooking water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Soaking and cooking chickpeas:

  1. Soak the chickpeas in water for 8 hours.
  2. After soaking, cook the chickpeas in a pressure cooker with 1.5 liters of water for 20 minutes.
  3. Once cooked, place the chickpeas in a container with cold water and remove the skins.

Blending the Ingredients:

  1. Place the cooked chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and a pinch of salt in a blender.
  2. Blend the ingredients while adding the chickpea cooking water until you reach the desired consistency.

Adjusting Seasoning:

  1. Taste the hummus and adjust the salt to taste.
  2. Serve and enjoy!
