My top 3 movies of all-time

Hobbit

My favorite movie of all time is the Hobbit. I love the story and the characters. I have watched it many times and I really never get tired of it.

Harry Potter

My second favorite movie is Harry Potter. I love the magic and the story. I have watched all the movies and I have read all the books.

Star Wars

My third favorite movie is Star Wars. I love the story and the characters. I have watched all the movies (I've also watched the Mandalorian) and I am a huge fan of Star Wars.
